About
CEQ “seek” partners institutional capital with investment opportunities in the upstream natural resources sector. As an operating partner, we can rapidly evaluate play concepts and identify the technical catalyst(s) in a potential project that may not be well understood or as easily recognized by traditional “financial buyers.”
Strategy
Pursue conventional development opportunities in targeted geographic areas where we can leverage our experience and networks. Identify underexploited assets that are uncompetitive for capital internally and candidates for divestment at large companies but that are high-return proposition for a lean, incentivized, low-cost operator. Capitalize on a prolonged recovery of prices and acceleration of capitulation driven divestitures from companies that deployed unprecedented amounts of capital at the top of a ten year commodities cycle.
